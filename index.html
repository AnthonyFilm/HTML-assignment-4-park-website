Sawgrass Lake Park

Sawgrass Lake Park sits on over 400 acres of beautiful Florida swamp land (that's real, not part of a con scheme). The park has over a mile of boardwalk trails, a two-story observation tower overlooking Sawgrass Lake, a picnic shelter, an environmental education center with an outdoor classroom, and plenty of cute creatures to observe (but not to mess with).
